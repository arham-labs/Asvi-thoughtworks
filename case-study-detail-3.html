Introduction

An 8-year-old organization, has been a pioneer in providing data science and AI ML models to address the last-mile customer needs related to data insights, analytics, and business decision-making. Having successfully served clients across multiple geographies and establishing a global presence, the company turned its focus inward to enhance its competitive advantage.

Business objectives

|

Design organization and team structures that promote internal mobility and collaboration.

|

Establish a career model that encourages employees to explore new roles within the organization.

|

Evaluate and benchmark roles to ensure internal consistency and recognition of competence.

Challenges faced

1

Managing a workforce from diverse cultures and domains

2

Maintaining a cohesive working environment and promoting a culture of teamwork

3

Attracting and retaining new talent, with opportunities for visible internal growth

Results and impact

1

Retention of new hires increased significantly after the implementation of the first phase of the project.

2

The model not only retained the internal eccentricity and creativity that defined the company but also made the company more competitive in the talent space.

3

The new structure has facilitated internal growth, job rotation, and short assignment models, ensuring a holistic talent development approach.
